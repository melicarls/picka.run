What is this?

picka.run is, simply put, a tool to help you pick a run. More specifically, it pulls your running activities from your Strava profile and matches them up with similar activities to create routes. picka.run calculates your average performance on each route, allows you to search your available routes by distance, and shows you a bunch of maps of options that you could use to hit your target distance for the day. If you don't have a Strava profile, you can click the 'Demo' button above to take a tour. Then, visit Strava to sign up for a profile! After you've logged a few runs with your GPS watch, smartphone, etc., come on back to picka.run, make a profile, and use this tool to help avoid route indecision the next time you lace up and hit the road.
